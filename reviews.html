Remedy Kitchen

<

Where: Royal Exchnage Arcade, Cross Street

The Remedy Kitchen is the first free from cafe in Manchester. Serving a gluten, dairy, wheat and refined sugar free menu so you don't feel so guilty after eating pancakes!

What: Pictured above is the Beetroot Latte, which didn't taste so much like coffee but had a warm wintery feel to it and come on who doesn't like a pink latte. I also ate vegan pancakes with homemade nutella and bananans. Honestly I never knew I needed 'homemade nutella' in my life before.

The staff at Remedy Kitchen are also super friendly and helpful and the cafe itself has the nicest atmosphere away from the busier cafes in the city
